Impact Story

"I just want everyone to have clean water like I do." – Rachel Beckwith

When 9-year-old Rachel Beckwith gave up birthday gifts to raise $300 for clean water, she never imagined what would happen next. After her sudden passing, her story inspired people around the world to donate in her honor—raising over $1.2 million. Thanks to Rachel’s selfless act, 37,000+ people in Ethiopia now have access to clean, safe drinking water.
